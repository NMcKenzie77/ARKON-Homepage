Your real estate ARKON build

The roles assigned to this business.

This is not the entire ARKON team. This is the practical build a real estate office would use first: calls, website leads, client follow-up, relationship memory, and owner visibility.

Voice reception

Vera

Answers inbound calls, captures buyer or seller details, and routes showing or listing requests when a person is needed.

Website leads

Porter

Captures website inquiries, answers approved questions, and turns casual interest into a handoff-ready lead.

Client follow-up

Naya

Follows up with leads and clients in the business voice so warm opportunities do not go cold.

Relationship memory

Marcus

Keeps lead history, notes, pipeline stage, prior touchpoints, and follow-up context attached.

Owner visibility

Grant

Shows what came in, what was handled, who owns the next step, and what needs attention.

Where it helps

Real estate work that should not slip.

Lead response

New buyer lead asks a question.

Porter captures the inquiry and Naya helps follow up before the lead goes cold.

Seller calls

A seller calls about timing.

Vera captures the reason for the call and routes anything requiring agent judgment.

Showing requests

A showing request comes in.

Details are collected cleanly so the agent starts with context instead of chasing information.

Agent handoff

An agent needs the history.

Marcus keeps prior touchpoints and notes attached before the callback or follow-up.

Example flow

How a lead moves through the build.

01

Lead comes from the website, a call, or a message.

02

Porter, Vera, or Naya responds based on the channel.

03

Marcus attaches contact history, notes, and pipeline context.

04

The agent gets a cleaner handoff and Grant keeps the owner informed.

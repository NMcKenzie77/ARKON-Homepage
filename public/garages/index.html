The shop reality

The repair order is not the whole relationship.

A customer may call about a noise, ask for a quote, approve part of the work, decline the rest, or walk out because the timing or price was not right that day. The shop may know the brakes, tires, battery, fluid leak, warning light, or maintenance item still needs attention, but follow-up gets buried when the next vehicle pulls in.

Deferred work is not dead work. ARKON helps keep recommended repairs, declined estimates, vehicle history, customer notes, status updates, service reminders, and follow-up context organized so the shop has more chances to bring the customer back.

The shop stays in control. ARKON supports the work around the repair, the customer, and the follow-up in the voice your shop approves.

Capture repair demand

Calls and estimate requests stay visible.

ARKON captures the customer, vehicle, issue, timing, contact details, urgency, and prior context so the front desk or advisor can follow up without starting cold.

Bring work back

Declined repairs do not disappear.

Recommended work, partial approvals, declined estimates, maintenance reminders, and customer-specific notes can stay in front of the shop instead of relying on memory.

Brief the owner

The owner sees what needs action.

ARKON surfaces missed calls, open estimates, status questions, deferred repairs, vehicle context, follow-up gaps, and customers who should be contacted again.

Prepared for shop review

ARKON also prepares the work around the vehicle decision.

ARKON does not diagnose vehicles, approve repairs, or replace the service advisor or technician. It answers in the shop's approved voice, gathers customer and vehicle context, organizes follow-up, prepares handoffs, and brings the right person in when the situation needs a person.

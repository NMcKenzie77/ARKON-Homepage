The owner reality

The front desk cannot stop when every chair is full.

A client may ask about price, availability, service time, stylist preference, deposits, policies, rescheduling, or same-day openings while the team is already working. A cancellation creates a gap. A no-show creates lost chair time. An unanswered message can become a lost booking.

A salon makes money when the chair is full. ARKON keeps appointment requests, client context, service questions, schedule changes, and follow-up organized so the owner is not carrying every loose end alone.

The salon stays in control. ARKON supports the work around the appointment, the client, and the calendar.

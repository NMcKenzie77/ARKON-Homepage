The owner reality

The appointment may be booked, but the relationship work is not finished.

A client may ask about price, availability, service time, stylist preference, deposits, policies, rescheduling, same-day openings, or whether they need a consultation before booking. A cancellation creates a gap. A no-show creates lost chair time. An unanswered message can become a lost client.

A salon makes money when the chair is full. ARKON keeps appointment requests, client context, photo intake, service questions, schedule changes, and follow-up organized so the owner is not carrying every loose end alone.

ARKON does not replace your booking system. It catches and organizes the conversations around the calendar.
